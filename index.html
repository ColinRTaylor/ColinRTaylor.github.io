Draft-Assist

Draft Assist was developed as a group project assignment for the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly. It was developed over a week-long sprint to simulate an NFL fantasy football draft. The server updates multiple clients in real time, to keep player selection, league teams, and the timer accurate.
